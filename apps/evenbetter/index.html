About Evenbetter

A small NZ consultancy with a serious global supply chain.

Evenbetter is led by Carter — an engineer who spends his time turning rough ideas into manufacturable parts. We pair hands-on design and reverse-engineering experience with direct relationships at two of China's most respected precision manufacturers.

The result: Kiwi businesses get factory-grade quality, sub-week lead times, and a single point of contact who actually understands the engineering — not just the freight forwarding.

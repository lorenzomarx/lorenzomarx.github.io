01 Officina · Our Story

Twenty years of European cars, in one West Auckland workshop.

Hello from West Euro Auto — a team of five based in West Auckland. Established in 2006, and a Motor Trade Association member since 2001.

We have a well maintained and fully equipped facility within which we have worked on a variety of cars doing a wide variety of jobs. Our experience is extensive.

Our specialty is Alfa Romeo and Fiat — full range of specialty tools and diagnostics — but every make and model is welcome. Experience on the floor ranges from ten years (the youngest member) to 38 years from our senior member.

There's a goldfish pond and a small garden out the back. We like the place tidy. We like the place ours.

02 Meet the Staff

The Foreman.

He has watched every job we've ever done. He keeps the floor honest. He does not bill in fifteen‑minute increments and he does not, as a rule, sign off on warrants.

The Foreman is the workshop's resident pigeon. He doesn't take instruction and he doesn't take leave. If you visit, you'll likely see him near the roller door. We ask one favour: please show him the respect he deserves.

House rule № 1

Be kind to The Foreman.

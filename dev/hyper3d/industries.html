Industries

Built for sectors where precision matters.

Engineering teams in regulated, high-stakes industries trust us with parts that have to perform exactly as drawn. Here are the sectors we know best.

ISO 13485

Medical & Biotech

Implants, surgical instruments, diagnostic equipment, lab automation and disposables. We work with regulated medical-grade materials including titanium Grade 23 ELI, 316L stainless, USP Class VI plastics and PEEK, with the documentation and traceability needed for ISO 13485, FDA and TGA submissions.

  • Orthopedic implants — knees, hips, spinal, trauma
  • Surgical instruments and trial trays
  • Diagnostic instrument housings and fluidics
  • Drug-delivery devices and disposables
  • Dental implants, abutments and prosthetics
  • Laboratory automation chassis and manifolds
IATF-aligned

Automotive & e-Mobility

Components for EVs, hybrids, motorsport, aftermarket and specialist vehicles. We support both prototype and series production, including PPAP-style documentation packages where required.

  • EV battery enclosures, brackets and busbars
  • Motor housings, end plates and rotor components
  • Charging connector bodies and cable assemblies
  • Aftermarket performance and motorsport parts
  • Test rigs and validation tooling
  • Interior trim, switches and HMI components
Precision-grade

Robotics & Automation

Structural and motion components for industrial robots, autonomous platforms, agricultural automation and material handling systems. Tight tolerances, surface finishes and material properties are critical — and we treat them accordingly.

  • Robot end-effectors, grippers and tool changers
  • Drive housings, gear cases and harmonic drive components
  • Frame, base and structural plate assemblies
  • Sensor mounts, optical brackets and calibration fixtures
  • Conveyor components and material-handling fixtures
  • Pneumatic manifolds and fluid power components

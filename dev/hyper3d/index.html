What we do

Four core capabilities, one accountable partner.

Whether you need a single machined prototype or a low-volume production run, we handle the manufacturing process end to end so you don't have to coordinate multiple suppliers.

CNC Machining

3-axis, 4-axis and 5-axis milling plus precision turning. Tolerances to ±0.01 mm across aluminium, steels, titanium and engineering plastics.

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Laser cutting, bending, welding and assembly. Enclosures, brackets, panels and chassis from stainless, aluminium, mild steel and copper.

View capability

Injection Moulding

Rapid tooling for prototypes through to production tooling. Engineering thermoplastics, over-moulding and insert moulding supported.

Post-Finishing

Anodising, powder coating, passivation, polishing, laser marking and DLC coating. Cosmetic and functional finishes done in-house.

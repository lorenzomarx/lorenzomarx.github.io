Capability

Sheet Metal Fabrication

Custom enclosures, brackets, panels and chassis — laser-cut, formed, welded and assembled to your drawing. Production-ready finishes done in-house.

Overview

From flat sheet to finished assembly.

Fibre laser cutting, CNC press braking, TIG and MIG welding, hardware insertion and final assembly — handled end to end. Your enclosure can leave the line powder-coated, screen-printed and with PEM hardware fitted, ready to install.

We supply both prototype quantities (1–10 parts) for design validation and ongoing production runs measured in thousands per month.

Specifications

What we can fabricate.

Material thickness

0.5 mm to 12 mm in steels and aluminium. Up to 6 mm in stainless steel.

Maximum sheet size

3000 × 1500 mm laser bed. Larger panels handled by sectioning and welding.

Tolerances

Cut features ±0.1 mm. Bends ±0.5° on standard parts, tighter on request.

Welding

TIG, MIG, spot and stitch welding. Stainless seam welding and aluminium fabrication.

Hardware

PEM nuts, studs and standoffs installed. Riveting, threaded inserts, hinges and gasketing fitted.

Finishing

Powder coating in any RAL colour, anodising, brushed finishes, screen printing and laser engraving.

Materials

Sheet stock we keep on hand.

Mild steel

Cold-rolled and hot-rolled in 0.8 mm to 12 mm. Ideal for brackets, frames and structural panels.

Stainless steel

304 and 316 in 0.5 mm to 6 mm. Brushed (#4) and 2B mill finishes for visible surfaces.

Aluminium

5052, 5083 and 6061 in 0.8 mm to 10 mm. Lightweight chassis, electrical enclosures and heat sinks.

Galvanised & pre-coated

Galvanised steel, Zincalume and pre-painted Colorsteel for outdoor and industrial enclosures.

Copper & brass

Sheet copper and brass for electrical bus bars, RF shielding and decorative architectural work.

Special alloys

Hastelloy, Inconel and bi-metal sheet on request for high-temperature or corrosive environments.

Typical parts

What we fabricate every week.

  • Industrial control cabinets and electrical enclosures
  • Server racks and 19" rack-mount chassis
  • EV charging station housings and weatherproof shells
  • Medical device covers, trolleys and equipment trays
  • Architectural panels, cladding and signage frames
  • Custom brackets, mounts and machine guards

The Ask

That the House of Representatives pass legislation to establish a mandatory public register of lobbyists, require proactive disclosure of meetings with Ministers and officials, and implement a “cooling-off” period for former Ministers and senior officials.

The Reform

Three pillars for public trust


  • 01 / 03
  • Register
  • A public list of who is paid to influence our politicians
  • Mandatory
  • Public and searchable
  • Who they lobby for
  • Updated regularly
  • Statutory framework

  • 02 / 03
  • Disclosure
  • Making it public when lobbyists meet with Ministers
  • Proactive reporting
  • Ministers & officials
  • No hidden meetings
  • Open by default

  • 03 / 03
  • Cooling-off
  • A rule that stops politicians walking into lobbying jobs the day they leave Parliament
  • Former Ministers
  • Senior officials
  • Closes the revolving door
  • Protects integrity

“We want to trust our politicians.”

So why won’t they tell us who they’re meeting with?
